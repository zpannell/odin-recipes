Beef Enchiladas

Description

This is a quick and simple five ingredient recipe for ground beef enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 jar 505 Southwestern salsa
  • 5 cups shredded Colby Jack cheese
  • 6 burrito sized tortillas
  • 1 15 oz. can Old El Paso enchilada sauce

Required Cookware

  • 9 x 13 baking pan
  • 12 inch skillet
  • Cooking grease (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Preheat skillet on medium-high heat
  3. Add beef to skillet and cook until browned
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low
  5. Drain skillet and place back on stove
  6. Add salsa and stir until uniformly distributed
  7. Add 3 ounces shredded cheese and stir evenly until melted
  8. Scoop some of beef, salsa, and cheese filling into a tortilla
  9. Roll into a burrito and place into a greased (optional) 9 x 13 baking pan
  10. Repeat until filling runs out
  11. Pour enchilada sauce over top of the enchiladas completely covering the top and sides
  12. Sprinkle remaining shredded cheese on top of enchiladas
  13. Place baking pan in oven for 16 minutes
  14. Let cool for three minutes and serve
