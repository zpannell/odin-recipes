Hot Dog

Description

A food consisting of a sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1 hot dog
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Ketchup (optional)

Required Cookware

  • A pot

Steps

  1. Fill pot halfway with water
  2. Boil water
  3. Once water is boiling, place hot dog in water
  4. Boil hot dog for 5 minutes
  5. Remove hot dog and place inside hot dog bun
  6. Optional: top with mustard and/or ketchup

Note: Always use caution when handling sharp objects and hot contents.
